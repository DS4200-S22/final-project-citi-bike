Bike Allocation Optimization for Citi Bike

Sky DeBoer, Tara Sawhney, Ritika Ponna, Harshit Sai Kannam, Amaya Kejriwal

Course Project as part of DS4200 S22: Information Visualization, taught by Prof. Ab Mosca, Data Visualization @ Khoury, Northeastern University.

Abstract

Our goal is to help Citi Bike optimize their bike allocations across New York City. In doing so, Citi Bike can focus on a big obstacle experienced by its users: finding docking space for their bikes after completing rides. This is demoralizing for users given high fees of keeping a bike longer than needed and time wastage. It is therefore important for Citi Bike management to understand their customer demographic and their docking needs.
